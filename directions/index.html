Venue & Accommodation

Venue

MAGIC 2026 will be hosted at the University of Glasgow in the West End of Glasgow.

There are convenient transport links including buses and trains into the city from the airport or a 20 minute journey by taxi.

Talks will be in the Joseph Black Building (School of Chemistry).

Accommodation

There will be a range of options for accommodation based on budget. There are 35 rooms available in university accommodation at a favourable rate.

We recommend booking this quickly when registration opens, as Glasgow is a very popular tourist destination, especially over the summer months.
